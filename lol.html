About Us

Riveras Institution is a non-profit organization focused on empowering pro-level young coders. We provide a platform where children can host advanced coding classes, set their own prices, and earn revenue while sharing their expertise.

We Give Certificates!

Our unique model encourages entrepreneurship among young developers, and those who complete our project-based exams successfully receive official certificates. These certificates recognize their achievements in coding and teaching others in a peer-driven environment.
